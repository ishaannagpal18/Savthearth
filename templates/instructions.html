EXAM INSTRUCTIONS

General Instructions

  1. The examination will comprise of objective type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
  2. All questions are compulsory and each carries One mark.
  3. There is no negative marking for the wrong answers.
  4. Once disqualified, you will not be eligible to appear for the savthearth quiz in the future run.
  5. The examination does not require using any paper, pen, pencil and calculator.
  6. Your desk/tables must be clear or any materials except your test-taking device.

Technical Instructions

  1. Switching tabs in between is strictly prohibited. After 10 WARNINGS you will be disqualified to appear for the test.
  2. You must use a functioning webcam and microphone.
  3. Turning on the camera is mandatory. The exam will be cancelled if you leave in between. The time limit is 30 mins for completing the exam.
  4. You are allowed to submit only once, make sure that you have correctly attempted all the questions before submission.
  5. The remaining time is shown in the Right Top Corner of the screen. It is mandatory to complete the test in time.
  6. Click on the submit button after completing the test to get the correct score.
  7. Do not leave the test in between. It will lead to cancellation of the test and you will have to restart the same.
  8. Do not reload the page, as it will lead to cancellation of exam.
