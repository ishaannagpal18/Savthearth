National Level Olympiad SAVTHEARTH

An initiative to educate and inform our youth about the environment.

Savthearth is about raising awareness among school students about the importance of protecting our planet and taking action. We hope to sensitize our youth to make the aware of the environmental situation.Â

Our Aim

The main goal of Savthearth has been to provide genuine educational information to youth in order to raise their level of awareness and teach them about the environment from a young age. It has organized several activities to improve the environmental quality of our planet.

CORE VALUES

  • Initiative to plant more trees
  • Easy reduction of energy
  • Recycling the waste
  • Conservation of water
  • Wise disposal of E-waste

Our Vision

We want to see a better, healthy and green tomorrow by transforming the future of the global environment. We aim to do so by sustainably and efficiently conserving, developing and utilizing natural resources.

Some Facts

SAVTHEARTH was founded in Delhi in 2019. In just 9 months, it had expanded to nearly ten cities. We have developed a more comprehensive plan and schedule for schools and their students to follow. They can even obtain various levels of certification.
